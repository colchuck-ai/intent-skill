# Modify outcome

**`MOD-OUT`**

You are **changing** an outcome document or its **index tables**. **Ripple** runs **down** to **requirements** (wording, scope, or presence) and **across** to **engineering** when the outcome’s promise no longer matches what is built.

**Context span:** outcome README, **product root** for alignment, **requirement** files under that outcome, and **engineering** as far as the change implies. This can be a large surface; load what the edit actually touches.

**Records:** **CRs** at outcome scope when the README or tables change meaningfully; **CRs** downstream when children must move. **PDRs** for **new** strategic decisions about the outcome.

---

### Draft

Example: narrowing an outcome might retire or rewrite requirement one-liners before deleting files—**Modify requirement** or **Remove requirement** may follow. Example: success metrics might force the engineering table to repoint.

### AI-assisted review

Check vertical consistency (root → outcome → requirements) and horizontal consistency with engineering. **CRs** and **PDRs** as the story demands.

### Independent review

Human gate on whether the outcome still matches the product story and whether the tree below was updated honestly.
